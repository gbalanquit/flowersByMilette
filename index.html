About Me

I have been decorating weddings and events for over a two decades. My designs are inspiring, creative and outright elegant for the appropriate occassions. My ability to create a color plan within the floral arrangements with my clients is essential to every aspect of the occassions . Flowers are essential for all aspects from wedding event, birthdays, and anniversaries. My ideas of floral placement in any event are dual duty (reuse) from the wedding to the reception brings your budget to affordability. My vision during planning stages will add elegance and whimsy to your amazing day. I can see beyond your vision and bring to you a fabulous romantic moment with the one you love.
